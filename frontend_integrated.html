Environmental Influence Rankings

These charts display the relative importance and SHAP-derived environmental influence rankings computed during model training. By cross-referencing daily weather records with actual sampling data, the machine learning models establish non-linear relationships to quantify how atmospheric events affect physical coal parameters. This intelligence powers the predictive alerts and operational recommendations across the platform.
